# Subscription Plans

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price | $0/mo | $29/user/mo | Custom |
| Included seats | 3 | 10 | Unlimited |
| API requests/min | 60 | 600 | 6,000 |
| Knowledge base articles | 50 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| SSO / SAML | No | No | Yes |
| Dedicated account manager | No | No | Yes |
| SLA | None | 99.5% uptime | 99.9% uptime |

## Upgrading Your Plan

Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**. Upgrades take effect
immediately, and you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of
the current billing cycle.

## Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. If your
current usage exceeds the limits of the lower tier (e.g., more seats than
included), you will be prompted to remove the excess before the downgrade
can be confirmed.

## Switching Between Monthly and Annual Billing

You can switch billing frequency at any time from **Settings → Billing →
Billing Frequency**. Switching to annual immediately applies the 15%
discount; switching from annual to monthly takes effect at the next
renewal date.

## Enterprise Custom Plans

Enterprise plans are tailored to usage volume, number of seats, and
required integrations (SSO, custom SLA, dedicated infrastructure).
Contact sales through the in-app "Talk to Sales" button or
enterprise@clouddesk.io to request a quote. Typical turnaround for a
custom quote is 1-3 business days.
