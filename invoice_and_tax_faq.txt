INVOICE AND TAX FAQ

DOES CLOUDDESK CHARGE TAX?

Tax is calculated based on your billing address and applicable local tax
laws (e.g., VAT in the EU, GST in India and Australia, sales tax in
certain US states). The applicable rate and amount are shown as a
separate line item on every invoice before checkout.

CAN I ADD MY COMPANY'S TAX ID / GSTIN / VAT NUMBER?

Yes. Go to Settings -> Billing -> Tax Information and enter your tax ID.
This will appear on all future invoices. It cannot be retroactively
added to invoices that were already generated; for those, contact
billing support to request a corrected invoice.

HOW DO I GET A RECEIPT FOR REIMBURSEMENT?

Every invoice in Settings -> Billing -> Invoice History can be downloaded
as a PDF that includes the company name, tax ID (if added), amount,
date, and payment method used. This is sufficient for most expense
reimbursement and accounting purposes.

WHY IS THE CHARGE AMOUNT DIFFERENT FROM MY PLAN PRICE?

This usually happens for one of these reasons:
- Proration from a mid-cycle upgrade or seat addition.
- API usage overage charges beyond your plan's included quota.
- Currency conversion if your card is billed in a different currency
  than your displayed plan price.
- Applicable tax added at checkout.

CAN I GET A CONSOLIDATED INVOICE FOR MULTIPLE TEAMS?

Enterprise customers managing multiple teams or sub-accounts can request
consolidated billing, which combines all team invoices into a single
monthly statement. This requires setup by our billing team — contact
your account manager.

DISPUTING A LINE ITEM ON AN INVOICE

If you believe a specific line item is incorrect (e.g., seat count that
doesn't match your actual team size), this requires manual review by a
billing specialist rather than self-service correction, since it
involves verifying account history.
