BILLING OVERVIEW

CloudDesk bills on a monthly or annual cycle depending on the plan you
selected at signup. This article explains how charges are calculated and
when they occur.

BILLING CYCLE

- Monthly plans are billed on the same calendar day each month as your
  original signup date. If you signed up on the 31st and a month has
  fewer days, billing occurs on the last day of that month.
- Annual plans are billed once per year and include a 15% discount versus
  paying monthly.
- All charges are processed automatically using the payment method on
  file under Settings -> Billing -> Payment Methods.

WHAT'S INCLUDED IN YOUR INVOICE

Your monthly invoice includes:
1. Base subscription fee for your plan tier (Free, Pro, or Enterprise).
2. Seat-based charges if you have added team members beyond your plan's
   included seats.
3. Usage overage charges, if applicable (e.g., API calls beyond your
   plan's included quota, calculated at $0.001 per extra call).
4. Applicable taxes based on your billing address.

PRORATION

If you upgrade or downgrade your plan mid-cycle, charges are prorated to
the day. Upgrading immediately applies the new plan and charges the
difference for the remaining days in the cycle. Downgrading takes effect
at the start of the next billing cycle to avoid mid-cycle service
disruption.

FAILED PAYMENTS

If a payment fails, CloudDesk retries the charge 3 times over 7 days. You
will receive an email notification after each failed attempt. If all
retries fail, the account is moved to a 14-day grace period with limited
functionality before suspension. Update your payment method under
Settings -> Billing to avoid interruption.

VIEWING PAST INVOICES

All invoices are available as downloadable PDFs under Settings -> Billing
-> Invoice History, going back to the start of your subscription.
