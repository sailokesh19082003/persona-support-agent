BILLING REFUND AND DISPUTE POLICY

ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUNDS

- Monthly subscriptions: refunds are available within 7 days of a charge
  if the product was not used (no logins, no API calls) during that
  billing period.
- Annual subscriptions: refunds are available within 30 days of the
  initial purchase on a pro-rated basis for unused months.
- Usage-based overage charges (API overage, extra seats already
  consumed) are non-refundable once incurred, since they reflect actual
  resource usage.

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

1. Go to Settings -> Billing -> Invoice History.
2. Select the relevant invoice and click "Request Refund."
3. Choose a reason from the dropdown (accidental purchase, duplicate
   charge, service issue, other).
4. Refund requests are reviewed by the billing team within 2 business
   days. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method
   and typically appear within 5-10 business days depending on your
   bank.

DUPLICATE CHARGES

If you believe you were charged twice for the same billing period, this
is typically caused by a retried payment after a temporarily declined
card that was later approved on both attempts. This is fully refundable
and should be reported immediately through the Invoice History page so
the duplicate can be identified and reversed.

CHARGEBACKS

We ask customers to contact CloudDesk support before initiating a
chargeback with their bank, since chargebacks can result in immediate
account suspension while the dispute is investigated, even if the
charge is later found to be valid. Most billing issues can be resolved
faster and without service disruption through a direct refund request.

ESCALATION

Any refund request involving a dispute over the legitimacy of a charge,
a chargeback already filed with a bank, or a request to delete billing
data must be escalated to a human billing specialist rather than handled
automatically, since these involve account-level financial decisions.
