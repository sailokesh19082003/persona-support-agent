CANCELING YOUR SUBSCRIPTION

HOW TO CANCEL

1. Go to Settings -> Billing -> Manage Subscription.
2. Click "Cancel Subscription."
3. Select a cancellation reason from the dropdown (this helps us improve
   the product).
4. Confirm cancellation. You will receive an email confirmation.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER CANCELING

- Your account remains active with full features until the end of the
  current billing period that you already paid for.
- After that date, the account converts to the Free plan automatically.
  No further charges will occur.
- Data is not deleted on cancellation. Tickets, knowledge base articles,
  and team settings are retained for 90 days in case you wish to
  reactivate.

REACTIVATING A CANCELED SUBSCRIPTION

You can reactivate at any time before the 90-day data retention window
ends by going to Settings -> Billing -> Reactivate Subscription. All
your historical data will be restored immediately.

CANCELING DUE TO A SERVICE ISSUE

If you are canceling because of an unresolved technical issue rather
than choice, we recommend opening a support ticket first describing the
issue. Many cancellations are avoidable once the underlying problem is
fixed, and our team can also offer service credits for extended outages
that affected your usage.

ACCOUNT DELETION (FULL DATA REMOVAL)

A full, permanent account deletion (removing all data immediately rather
than the standard 90-day retention) is a separate, irreversible action
and must be requested through a human support specialist to confirm
identity and intent before proceeding.
