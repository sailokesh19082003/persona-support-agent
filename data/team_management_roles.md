# Team Management and Roles

## Available Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| Owner | Full access including billing, deleting the account, and transferring ownership |
| Admin | Manage team members, integrations, and security settings; no billing access |
| Member | Create and respond to tickets, view knowledge base; cannot change settings |
| Viewer | Read-only access to tickets and reports |

A workspace can have multiple Owners and Admins, but billing actions
(canceling, changing payment method) require Owner-level access
specifically.

## Inviting Team Members

1. Go to **Settings → Team → Invite Members**.
2. Enter their email and select a role.
3. They'll receive an invite email valid for 7 days. If it expires,
   resend it from the same page.
4. New members count against your plan's included seats; additional
   seats beyond the included amount are billed per the per-seat rate on
   your plan.

## Changing a Member's Role

Only Owners and Admins can change roles, and Admins cannot promote
someone to Owner. Go to Settings → Team → click the member → change
role in the dropdown.

## Removing a Team Member

Removing a member immediately revokes their access. Their past activity
(tickets they handled, comments) remains in the system attributed to
their name for audit purposes, but their login no longer works.

## Transferring Ownership

Ownership transfer requires the current Owner to initiate it from
Settings → Team → Transfer Ownership, and the new Owner must accept via
an emailed confirmation link. This is a sensitive action that affects
billing control and account-level permissions, so it always requires
explicit two-step confirmation and cannot be done on behalf of a user by
support staff without identity verification.
