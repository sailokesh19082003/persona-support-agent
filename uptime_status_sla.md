# Uptime, Status Page, and SLA

## Status Page

Live system status, ongoing incidents, and scheduled maintenance windows
are published at **status.clouddesk.io**. Subscribe there for email or
SMS alerts whenever an incident is opened or resolved.

## SLA by Plan

| Plan | Uptime Commitment | Service Credit for Breach |
|---|---|---|
| Free | None | N/A |
| Pro | 99.5% monthly uptime | 10% of monthly fee per 1% below commitment |
| Enterprise | 99.9% monthly uptime | 25% of monthly fee per 1% below commitment, custom terms available |

## What Counts as Downtime

Downtime is defined as the API or web application returning error rates
above 5% or average response times above 10 seconds, sustained for more
than 5 consecutive minutes, as measured by our independent third-party
monitoring service. Scheduled maintenance windows (announced at least 48
hours in advance) do not count toward downtime calculations.

## Requesting a Service Credit

If you believe an incident caused your account's uptime to fall below
your plan's SLA commitment for a billing period:

1. Open a ticket referencing the specific incident (linked from the
   status page) and your account ID.
2. Our team cross-references the incident timeline against your usage
   logs to confirm impact.
3. Approved credits are applied to your next invoice, not refunded as
   cash.

## Scheduled Maintenance

Routine maintenance is performed in low-traffic windows and announced at
least 48 hours in advance via the status page and email to account
Owners and Admins. Emergency security patches may occur with shorter
notice when necessary to protect customer data.
